MYSP In Action aims to capture and share best practices happening across the system that directly support the 2024-2028 Multi-Year Strategic Plan (MYSP) strategic directions. With Equity as a guiding principle, the MYSP In Action initiative will showcase best practices in instructional strategies and school and student initiatives to recognize excellence in TDSB schools, share ideas and inspire action.

To start, MSYP in Action will focus on showcasing best practices connected to the strategic direction of Belong, which aims to ensure all students belong, are engaged, and feel valued in an inclusive environment. Check out the best practices below to see how TDSB staff are taking action to foster equity, well-being and connection to create the conditions necessary for student engagement, belonging, and success.

Fit-Fun-Friday: Bringing the School Together to Building Belonging
ABC School (Department)
Grade 1-8 Students
2023-2024 School Year
keyboard_arrow_down
What?
The Student Well-Being and Belonging Mental Health Ambassador Program is a collaborative initiative involving 10 students from 5 different schools. This program aims to empower young leaders to promote mental health awareness, support, and inclusivity within their school communities. Each student ambassador is trained to provide peer support, organize mental health workshops, and foster a safe and welcoming environment. dedicated to student well-being.
kid_star
Impact
By facilitating open conversations and coordinating events, these ambassadors help reduce stigma and enhance a sense of belonging. The program encourages cross-school networking and resource sharing, creating a supportive network. To date this initiative has held 5 well being workshops for students and the students have created a mental health workbook in May 2024 that’s already been accessed over 500 times!
sentiment_very_satisfied
WOW!
What are your words of wisdom for adopting this practice?
Empower student ambassadors with a deep understanding of mental health, active listening, and peer support, ensuring they receive ongoing guidance and access to professional resources when needed.

Encourage regular communication and collaboration among ambassadors across schools, fostering connection and unity through meetings, online platforms, and shared events, where ideas and strategies can flourish.

Engage the wider school community—teachers, parents, and students—by raising awareness of the program’s mission through newsletters, social media, and events, creating a supportive environment that promotes mental well-being and helps reduce the stigma surrounding mental health.
